The Changing Face of Heroin

America's prescription painkiller epidemic has fueled an unexpected rise in another addictive drug: heroin. See how the demographics of heroin users have shifted over time as the demand for this drug has skyrocketed.

The State of Syringe Exchange

The availability of needle exchanges has the potential to mitigate the spread of diseases transmitted by injection drug use and needle sharing. Given that between 2011 and 2013 the estimated number of new cases of hepatitis C rose from 16,500 to 29,700, this issue is as important as ever.

Tweeting About Heroin

Heroin use in the United States has been growing at an alarming rate in recent years, and the prevalence of this drug is reflected in the conversations being held on Twitter. We found over 16,000 tweets from a recent 12-month period that mentioned heroin, and examined the data to find out more about who these people are and what they are saying about this deadly drug.
